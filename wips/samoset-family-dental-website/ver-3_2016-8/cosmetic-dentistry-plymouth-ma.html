Composite White Fillings

The days of amalgam or silver fillings are coming to an end. White fillings are placed for many reasons, whether it is to patch a broken tooth or an area of decay, we can provide you with an aesthetically-pleasing and excellently-functioning filling.

What can I expect during my appointment?

Dr. Sheng will place your new filling in one visit. She begins by preparing the affected area. Next, she cleans the prepped area and places a bonding material to adhere the white composite material to the tooth and insulate the tooth from extreme temperature changes. Then she will mix the filling composite material with coloring to match it to your true tooth color. Finally, she applies and shapes the color-matched composite material before polishing and smoothing the tooth to restore it to its original shape and function.

Once the white filling is done it will blend in with the rest of your teeth seamlessly, you won’t even notice the difference!

Lifetime Whitening

Our Lifetime Whitening program is an offer to ensure your teeth can stay whitened lifelong at a minimum cost. We make a custom tray with your teeth’s impressions and unlimited tubes of whitening material refilled every cleaning visit.

What can I expect during my appointment?

You begin with a brief appointment with our hygienist. She will take impressions of your teeth and document your current tooth shade, so that we can keep track of your progress! You then return within a week for your own custom trays tailored for your mouth and a whitening kit. We will educate you on how to effectively use the whitening materials. Then for every future cleaning visit we will address any concerns you may have and give you a refill of whitening material at no charge so you can maintain your desired smile!

Every five years you are entitled to fresh trays to ensure the maximum comfort and effectiveness at no cost!
