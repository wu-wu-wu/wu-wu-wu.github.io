Hygienists & Staff

Sue Nogueira

Frontdesk

Sue is the first smiling face you’ll meet in our office. She will help you figure out your insurance coverage, file your dental claims to and get the most benefit from your insurance companies. In addition to being the friendliest frontdesk she has also worked as a dental assistant for over 20 years - meaning she's equipped with chairside experience and patient education. When she's not commanding the frontdesk you can find Sue making absolutely adorable hand-craft crochet pieces.

Jean Lindberg, RDH

Hygienist

Jean has over 20 years of experience as a hygienist. She graduated from the New Hampshire Technical Institute. As a mother of seven she is incredibly patient, caring and professional and can put any anxious patient at ease. Coming in for one of her gentle cleanings will feel more like a spa treatment than a dental visit! In her spare time she is busy running a household and loving her family.

Yolanda Patton, RDH

Hygienist

Yolanda has nearly 30 years of experience hygienist. She received her license from the Forsyth School of Dental Hygiene. Once her patients gets in the chair before long they are chatting like they're both long time friends. Yolanda is trilingual in English, Spanish and Portuguese. On her days off Yolanda likes spending time with her family, gardening and dancing (she's great at weddings).

Rylee Burt

Assistant

Rylee is a licensed Dental Assistant. She graduated with honors from Lincoln Technical Institute and was awarded the “Most Outstanding Student” in her year. She has worked in a preschool for six years and brings to our office her invaluable empathy and ability to work with kids and adults alike. When she’s not assisting she’s either spending time with loved ones or coaching softball.

Suzanne Wu

Designer

Suzanne received her BFA from the Rhode Island School of Design with a focus on human interactions. She is improving our patient experience by making it easier for patients to reach us and find information. This website you're browsing is designed and coded by her! When she's not in the office Suzanne is either learning something new, spending time with friends or volunteering her design skills for various nonprofits.
