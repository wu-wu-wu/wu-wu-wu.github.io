Our Mission

Here at Samoset Family Dental, we are continuing the legacy of Dr. Stephen Horn with our family-friendly office and warm staff. Over the past 40 years, Dr. Horn has cultivated an atmosphere where everyone can feel welcome and each individual is provided for with full care and consideration for their unique situations.

Since Dr. Sheng joined the practice, we have been growing our office and expanding our service offered in an effort to provide you with everything you need to remain healthy. From general dentistry to specialized services, our team will provide you with the up-to-date, affordable and gentle care for all your dental need.
