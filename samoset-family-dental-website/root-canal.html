Root Canal Therapy

Here we have the capability and equipment to provide you with any RCT treatment you may need.

We seek to ease the typical negative associations made with RCT we assure each patient that we do everything within our power to make the procedure as pleasant as possible. Technology offers us the ability to sedate your tooth and the surrounding anatomy so that you will be able to lay back and relax during the procedure.

Visit 1:

Typically a RCT will consist of two visits. The first visit will be the actual RCT procedure where we remove the offending nerve and tissue and finish by packing the space with infection stopping material.

Visit 2:

The next visit we will place a post and core in the tooth that will allow you to have the strength needed for the tooth to continue in its function for you.
