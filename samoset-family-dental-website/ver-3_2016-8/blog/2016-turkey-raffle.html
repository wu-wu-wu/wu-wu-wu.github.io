Thanksgiving Turkey Raffle

Are You One of Our Lucky Patients to Bring Home a Turkey this Thanksgiving?

As fall approaches fast we look back on our year well spent with our patients. We are thankful to you, our loyal patients for choosing us as your dental care providers. As a thank you we are giving away five free butterball turkeys to five lucky patients!

To Participate:

In order to be included as a part of the drawing you need to have:

  1. Received a dental visit between May and mid-November
  2. Write your name and put it inside our Turkey Raffle box in our office
  3. (Optional) A donation to go to the Plymouth Council On Aging - to help elders in our community.

So on come in and get your name in before the deadline - we will be drawing for the winners on November 14th, 2016!
