Dr. Stephen Horn

Dr. Horn received his undergraduate degree at Boston University. He then attended the Loyola University School of Dentistry where he was inducted into the Blue Key Honor Society.

In order to stay current with new dental methods and technologies, Dr. Horn annually takes many hours of continued education. He is a member of the Sea Coast Esthetic Dentistry Association. Through this professional group, he learns the latest in cosmetic and implant dentistry.

In his leisure time, Dr. Horn enjoys fishing, kayaking and photography. His photos frequently come from his trips with his wife, whom he's been married to for forty-three years. Some of his photos can be seen hanging on the office walls. His photography collection is on www.stevehorn.info.
